High Risk/Motorcycle

If applicable, complete the following items for High Risk Activities.
High Risk Activites are activities having a higher potential for injury. Some examples include flying civil aircraft, hang gliding, skydiving, parasailing, ATV riding, snowboarding, rock climbing rappelling, skiing, and similar activities.

    • Review, digitally sign, then email to Flight Commander
  • Motorcycle/ATV Riders
    • Form 708 and Motorcycle Rider ORM Score Card
    • Riders must always wear proper personal protective equipment IAW AFI 91-207 Para 3.4.2.4.
    • Before riding, receive Flt/CC approval and Safety Rider briefing from you squadron's Motorcycle Safety Rep.
