Mandatory In-Processing Briefings

All new members must attend the following briefings within 60 calendar days from DAS. Submit your name to schedule your briefings within 3 duty days.

  • Newcomer's Briefing
    • Every second Tuesday from 0730-1100 in the Professional Development Center, Bldg 455.
    • Spouses are welcome and encouraged to attend.
  • Medical Right Start
    • Turn in medical records to Base Clinic within 3 duty days.
